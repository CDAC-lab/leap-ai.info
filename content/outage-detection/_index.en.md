## Outage Detection

LEAP's Outage Detection capability provides continuous, automated surveillance of energy infrastructure across La Trobe's five campuses — identifying faults rapidly and delivering the actionable intelligence needed for fast response.

### Fleet Health & Meter Status

LEAP monitors the health of every building meter and solar meter in the fleet in real time. A unified fleet health dashboard surfaces the status of each asset — flagging meters that are offline, underperforming, or reporting anomalous data — so facilities managers can prioritise their attention without manually interrogating individual systems.

Key indicators include connection status, data recency, and deviation from expected baselines. System-wide anomalies (affecting multiple meters or zones simultaneously) are distinguished automatically from isolated individual faults, enabling faster triage.

![Outage Detection fleet health dashboard showing meter status across the campus fleet](/images/leap/outage-detection/fleet-health.png)

### Automatic Outage Classification

When an outage event is detected, LEAP's AI engine automatically classifies it into one of several outage types — including full supply interruptions, partial load drops, sensor failures, and solar generation gaps. This removes the burden of manual diagnosis and gives operations teams a head start on remediation.

### Actionable Outage Reports

Each detected outage generates a structured, human-readable report containing:

- The affected meter(s) and building location
- Outage type classification
- Time of detection and estimated duration
- Contextual comparison against historical behaviour
- Recommended next steps for investigation

These reports can be generated on demand through the **COOEE AI Companion**, making the data accessible to non-technical stakeholders without requiring dashboard access.

![Actionable outage report showing system-wide events, individual outages, key findings and severity classification](/images/leap/outage-detection/outage-report.png)

### Outage Analytics & Trend Analysis

Beyond individual events, LEAP aggregates outage history into analytics views that reveal recurring fault patterns, seasonal trends, and asset-level reliability profiles. Severity scoring ranks issues by operational impact, helping prioritise infrastructure investment and maintenance scheduling.

This longitudinal perspective supports La Trobe's broader net zero program by identifying systemic inefficiencies and quantifying the energy cost of unplanned outages.
